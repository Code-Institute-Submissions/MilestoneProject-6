Hair O' The Dog Club History

Hair O' The Dog MCC was founded in 2004 by Ronnie Robinson (Rocket) and James Noone (Speedy). The club was named after a bar in Moville, Donegal, with the same name who's owner was also a biker. The aim of the club was to create a more relaxed motorcycle club than your average MC or MCC. While member participation is important in HOTD, it's not as strict on the minimum number of rallies that must be attended in any given year. All club members are resident in Ireland and try to meet up as often as possible for nights out, group rides and charity events. A fun, family friendly, laid back MCC that just want to have the craic.

Meet the Members

Rocket

Rocket is one of the founders of HOTD. He currently rides a Pan European and if you're ever on the road with him, you'll understand how he got his name when you see a sudden blur out of the corner of your right eye.

Speedy

Speedy is one of the founders of HOTD. When he's not on his bike, he has the pace of a tortoise and is often late to the party. While the latter may be true, he will always be the last one standing.

Taco

Taco is the club secretary and is one the First Six. He has a love for cheap cider and fast bikes but above all he has a peculiar love of taco sauce. Around two hours after beer-o-clock this man would eat anything, as long as it has a sparing amount of taco sauce doused on it.

Batman

Batman is one of the First Six. By day he is the fun-loving family man, by night he is the saviour of Gotham Ballybofey city!

The Gent

The Gent is the club treasurer and one of the First Six. This guy is the perfect gentleman when out and about at rallies. Well until about 9pm, then he needs carried to his tent. Sir cannot handle his wine.
