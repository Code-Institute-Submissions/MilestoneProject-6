Hair O' The Dog MCC

We are a group of like minded motorcycle enthusiasts who love the open road and everything motorcycles. We all come from different backgrounds, have different views and opinions but were all brought together by our love of two wheels.
HOTD is currently accepting new members who do not take life too serious, have a passion for motorbikes and just want to join a laid back MCC. Members are expected to participate in a few rallies and meetings each year but we understand life can be busy at times, therefore we do not penalise members for not attending events. If you are interested in joining, why don't you leave your details on our contact form and we will get back to you.
We frequently organise ride outs and charity events which can be booked through our website. If you'd like to keep up to date with our activites, why not give us a like on social media by clicking the icons in the footer of this Website.
